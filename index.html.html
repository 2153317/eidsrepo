Eid Greeting
عيد مبارك وعيد سعيد
Wishing you a blessed and joyful Eid! May this sacred day fill your heart with happiness, your home with laughter, and your life with the endless light of Allah's blessings.
🤲
Dua of Acceptance
تَقَبَّلَ اللَّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنْكُمْ
"Taqabbalallāhu minnā wa minkum" — May Allah accept from us and from you. This beloved dua has been exchanged among Muslims since the era of the Companions (Sahaba).
☀️
Dua for Blessings
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِي أَيَّامِنَا
"Allāhumma bārik lanā fī ayyāminā" — O Allah, bless us in our days. May every moment of this Eid and each day ahead overflow with Your divine grace and endless mercy.
📖
Quranic Dua
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً
"Rabbanā ātinā fid-dunyā ḥasanah, wa fil-ākhirati ḥasanah, wa qinā 'adhāban-nār" — Our Lord! Give us good in this world and the Hereafter, and guard us from the Fire. (Quran 2:201)
💚
Annual Blessing
كل عام وأنتم بخير
May every year find you in good health, happiness, and the warm company of loved ones. May Allah shower you with His endless mercy and keep you smiling always.
🕊️
Dua for Forgiveness
اللَّهُمَّ اغْفِرْ لَنَا وَارْحَمْنَا وَعَافِنَا
"Allāhumma-ghfir lanā, wa-rḥamnā, wa-'āfinā" — O Allah, forgive us, have mercy on us, and grant us well-being. May this Eid be a fresh start of piety and closeness to Allah.
✦ Grand Supplication ✦
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ كَرِيمٌ
تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنَّا
Allāhumma innaka 'afuwwun karīmun, tuḥibb-ul-'afwa fa'fu 'annā
"O Allah, You are Pardoning and Generous; You love to pardon, so pardon us." — Sunan Ibn Mājah · Recited abundantly on Eid and the blessed nights
